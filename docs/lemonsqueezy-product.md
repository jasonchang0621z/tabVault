## Description

**Too many tabs? TabVault saves them all.**

TabVault is a Chrome extension that saves your entire browser window — every tab and tab group — as a named workspace. Close your tabs, switch to a different project, and restore everything later with a single click. Tab groups come back with their original colors, names, and collapsed state.

**What Pro unlocks:**

The free version lets you save up to 3 workspaces. TabVault Pro removes the limit and adds power features:

- **Unlimited workspaces** — save as many as you need, no cap
- **Tab group restore** — bring back tab groups with colors, names, and collapsed state intact
- **Export & Import** — back up your workspaces as JSON, share them across devices, or migrate to a new computer
- **Auto-backup** — automatically saves your workspace once a day so you never lose anything

Yes. Your license key works on multiple Chrome installations. Each device activates independently.

After purchase, you'll receive a license key. Open the TabVault extension, click "Unlock Pro", and paste your key. That's it.
